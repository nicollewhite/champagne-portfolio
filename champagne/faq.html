Frequently Asked Questions


Here are some additional details you may have questions about. If you have other questions, please contact either one of us.


Q. What's the dress code?

A. Cocktail reception, semi-formal wear. It's a special occasion - so dress to look and feel good!


Q. It's a birthday party - do I bring you a present?

A. We are not expecting birthday gifts at our party. We'd much prefer you sharing this special day with us!


Q. What are the dinner details?

A. There will be food available throughout the night, but no formal sit down dinner will be happening.


Q. There are drink tickets - can I have more to drink after?

A. Absolutely! If you'd like to continue drinking after using your drink tickets, all beverages under $10 will be $2 for you! Please drink responsibly.


Q. I live far, is anyone staying the night?

A. We have a deal with the hotel for single and double rooms that night. Up to four people can share a room for the same great price of $289 a night (tax not included). Please use the link to book a room if you'd like to stay! No drinking and driving!!
